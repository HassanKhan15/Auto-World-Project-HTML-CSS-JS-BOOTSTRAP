Toyota Prado

The 4th generation of Toyota Land Cruiser Prado was launched in 2009. Globally Prado is being offered in 5-door and 3-door versions but in Pakistan only 5-door version is available. The Toyota Land Cruiser Prado 2017 is being offered in 3 versions TX 3.0D(Diesel), VX 3.0(Diesel) and VX 4.0(Petrol). TLC Prado has always been famous in Pakistan. Price of the 4th generation Toyota Land Cruiser significantly increased as compare to the previous generation. Toyota Land Cruiser Prado 2017 price in Pakistan is approx13.9 million to 14.9 million which is fairly competitive when compared to other models in the same class.
