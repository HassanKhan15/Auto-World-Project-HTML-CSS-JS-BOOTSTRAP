Toyota Hilux

Toyota Hilux Revo, often termed as Toyota Revo, is manufactured by Toyota. Known for its reliability, Toyota Revo was launched worldwide in 2015. The variants of Toyota Hilux Revo are divided into the single cabin and double cabin models. The Toyota Revo 2017 Pakistan has kept up with its strong nature by providing the best performance in off-road and on-road environments. Known as the toughest 4x4, Toyota Hilux Revo 2017 can be tuned and structurally enhanced for almost any kind of operation. Toyota Hilux Revo 2017 price in Pakistan is about PKR 2,144,000 To 4,399,000.

Exterior

Toyota Revo 2017 is inspired from the guidelines of “Keen look” design language. Toyota Revo price in Pakistan is dependent upon the features. However, it is expected to cost between PKR 3.8 million and PKR 4.5 million. New curved wheel arches and upgraded front fascia give Toyota Revo 2016 a more muscular and giant look. LED-equipped headlights are another addition. Front bumper is also updated and is more pronounced than the previous generation. Toyota Pakistan is also selling scores of exterior updates like front bulbar, rear bulbar, hunting lights, toolbox, etc.

Interior
Toyota Hilux Revo 2017 has a simple and minimalistic interior to cater a certain market. Toyota has tried to give Hilux Revo a more modern look. The car has push start button, a touchscreen LCD, and climate control. It also has a cooler box, which can store two 600ml water bottles. Adjustable Steering Wheel – Tilt & Telescopic, Cruise Control, Mobile Phone Connectivity, Power Steering, Trip Computer, and Voice Recognition System are other salient features of new Revo.
